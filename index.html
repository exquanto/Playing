The laser, the transistor and integrated circuit all have their origins in quantum physics and are applications of the first quantum revolution.

The second quantum revolution starting now to unfold worldwide brings transformative advances to science, industry and society. It creates new commercial opportunities for computation and cryptography but also for energy generation and use, high resolution sensing and imaging, simulation.

At the same time its foundations are highly counterintuitive and this is a major obstacle for managers and investors towards the inevitable incorporation of these technologies in current product lines or for new business models that they make possible.

ExQuanto's purpose is to facilitate the transition from the lab to real life applications.
